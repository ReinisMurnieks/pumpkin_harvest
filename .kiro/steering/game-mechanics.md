# Game Mechanics Documentation

## City Metrics

### Population
- Generated by residential buildings
- Houses: 15 people
- Apartments: 40 people
- Generates tax income

### Budget
- Starting budget: $50,000
- Income sources:
  - Population taxes (10% rate)
  - Commercial buildings ($500/month)
  - Industrial buildings ($800/month)
  - Stadiums ($1000/month)
- Expenses:
  - Building maintenance costs
  - Monthly upkeep

### Happiness (0-100%)
**Positive factors:**
- Parks (+5% each)
- Hospitals (+15% each)
- Schools (+12% each)
- Police stations (reduce crime)
- Low pollution
- Low traffic
- Full employment

**Negative factors:**
- Power shortage (-20%)
- Water shortage (-20%)
- Unemployment (-10 to -20%)
- High pollution (-15%)
- High crime (-15%)
- High traffic (-10%)

### Power System
- Supply: Power plants (100 units each)
- Demand: All buildings consume power
- Shortage = unhappy citizens

### Water System
- Supply: Water towers (100 units each)
- Demand: All buildings need water
- Shortage = unhappy citizens

### Employment
- Jobs provided by:
  - Commercial: 20 jobs
  - Industrial: 30 jobs
  - Hospitals: 15 jobs
  - Schools: 10 jobs
  - Police: 12 jobs
  - Power plants: 8 jobs
  - Water towers: 5 jobs
  - Stadiums: 25 jobs
- Unemployment = population - total jobs

### Pollution
- Sources: Industrial buildings (+10), factories
- Reducers: Parks (-3 each)
- Affects happiness

### Crime
- Base crime: 10
- Increased by: Buildings, unemployment
- Reduced by: Police stations (-20), schools (-8), parks (-2)

### Traffic
- Generated by: All buildings
- Reduced by: Roads (-5), Metro stations (-10)

## Day/Night Cycle
- 24-hour cycle (0-24)
- Day: 6 AM - 6 PM
- Night: 6 PM - 6 AM
- Building lights turn on at night
- Speed adjustable (1x, 5x, 15x)

## Metro System
- Requires 2+ metro stations
- Trains automatically connect all stations
- Reduces traffic significantly
- Passengers simulated
- Multiple trains can run simultaneously
